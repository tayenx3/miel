# Contributing to Miel

THANK YOU THANK YOU THANK YOU THANK YOU THANK YOU THANK YOU THANK YOU\
for considering contributing to Miel!

---

However, Miel is a labor of love with a specific vision. 
That vision belongs to me (the creator).

Here's how feature decisions work:

1. **Open a discussion** on GitHub Issues
2. **Make your case** with examples, use cases, and trade-offs
3. **I will listen** and engage honestly
4. **I will decide** based on Miel's vision (cozy, capability-aware, affine types)
5. **If I say no**, you can:
   - Accept it and keep using Miel
   - Fork Miel and make your own dialect (seriously, I'll link to it)
   - Leave (no hard feelings)

## So what makes Miel *Miel*?

Before proposing a change, understand these non-negotiables:

1. **Cozy over competitive** - No features solely for the "Rust killer" title
2. **Capability types** - `acquire`, `@Cap`, etc. stay
3. **Affine types** - Move semantics, automatic drops
4. **`PascalCase` and `camelCase`** - Part of Miel's "coziness"

If your proposal contradicts these, please fork instead.
